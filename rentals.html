Rent the Grinter Visitor Center for Events!

Grinter Event Space

Perfect for weddings, family reunions, and more.

Rental Details

A $200 security deposit is required for all rentals, refundable upon event completion. Rental fees start at $100 per hour, with special offers on extended rentals. A contract and rental agreement are mandatory.

  • Friday & Saturday: 4-hour minimum, extended hours available.
  • Sunday-Thursday: 3-hour minimum.
Contact Us for Booking

Amenities

Fully equipped caterer’s kitchen

Tables, chairs, and supplies provided

Picnic Shelter Information

The outdoor picnic shelter features electric lights, outlets, and flush toilets. Rental is $50 per day.

Alcohol Policy

If alcohol is served, an insurance policy is required. We recommend theeventhelper.com for coverage.
